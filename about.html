About The Artist

Hi my name is Zikisa Cale Oasin Sogaxa, growing up in a place where technology wasn't really a thing, there were no tvs or cellphones, I only relied on my imagination, because of that I did not see the world the same way an average person does.

I always imagined my eyes were paint brushes, the worlds fastet painter, drawing the world and sending the image to my brain to keep, like how cameras print light to paper. I started drawing at a very young age, in school instead of writing notes I would draw on my notebook, putting in picture whatever is on my mind on paper.

Like any other child growing up I started drawing stick figures, round head with lines for arms and legs, but I did not want to stop there Iknew I could make my drawings look much more realistic, so I would explore mouth shapes and yes, adding shoes and clothes to my stick figures, I practised till I was fairly good at it.
Than I thought how about start drawing from an image, I was really not good at it, but the reaction I got from the people who saw the drawing really liked it and complimented me and that made me fall inlove with art.

I started exploring different kinds of drawing techniques like scribble art, doodling and my favourite, stipple art. When I first saw stippled sketch I couldn't believe it, a drawing made form just thousands and thousands of dots is just amazing, and since I was not that good a shading my images stippling really helped a lot to bring life to my sketches.
